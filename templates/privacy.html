Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using Spendly. This includes your account information (email address), expense data (amounts, categories, dates, descriptions), and any other information you choose to enter into the application. We do not collect or store sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers or bank account details.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely to operate, maintain, and improve the Spendly service. This includes generating expense reports, categorizing transactions, displaying spending trends, and providing you with insights into your financial habits. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We recommend regularly backing up your data and using strong, unique passwords for your account.

Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or other operational purposes. These third parties may have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information. We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export your expense data from your account settings. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the application or via email at least 30 days before the new policy takes effect. Your continued use of Spendly after the changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application. We are committed to addressing your concerns in a timely and transparent manner.
